## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [foryoueats.com](https://foryoueats.com/register) using your email, Google, Facebook, or Apple account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first recipe">
    Paste a URL from any recipe website to import it instantly, snap a photo of a cookbook page, or create a recipe from scratch using the recipe editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start cooking">
    Check your recipe, create a shopping list, get your ingredients and get to cooking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Already using another app?

Switching from another recipe manager like Plan To Eat, Paprika, or Copy Me That? You can bring your recipes with you using our bulk import tool. We support a growing list of apps — and if yours isn't on the list yet, let us know and we'll do our best to add it.
